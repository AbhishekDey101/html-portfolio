The Best Movies of All Time by Abhishek

My top 3 Movies of All Time

Life In A Metro

Set against the backdrop of Mumbai in the early
2000s, the movie shows the intricacies of relationships
in a fast moving metropolis

Kapoor and Sons: Since 1929

A look at life and relationships through the perspective
of two brothers and their family, set in the sleepy and
rolling hills of Coonoor.

Wake up Sid

With the Mumbai of the early 2000s as a backdrop, a look
at the rather elusive acts of adulting and romantic
relationships through the eyes of Sid.
